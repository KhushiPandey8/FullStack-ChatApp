## Reporting a Vulnerability

To report a security vulnerability in the **Full-Stack Chat App**, please follow these steps:

1. **Email us at:** [security@example.com](mailto:security@example.com) with details of the issue.
2. **Provide a clear description** of the vulnerability, including:
   - Steps to reproduce the issue
   - Potential impact and affected components
   - Any suggested mitigation or patches
3. **Confidentiality:** We encourage responsible disclosure and request that you **do not** publicly disclose the issue until we have addressed it.
4. **Response Time:**
   - Acknowledgment within **48 hours**.
   - Assessment and triage within **5 business days**.
   - Fix deployment based on severity:
     - **Critical:** Patch released within **7 days**.
     - **High:** Patch released within **14 days**.
     - **Medium/Low:** Addressed in the next scheduled release.
5. **Bounty Program:** Security researchers who responsibly disclose critical vulnerabilities may be eligible for a bounty reward.
